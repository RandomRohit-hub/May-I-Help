NOTICE

Lightning and Environmental Safety

Never operate the MMTT handheld device outdoors during thunderstorms or in high-risk lightning environments. The antenna and the user may form a conductive path that increases the risk of injury.

If severe weather approaches, switch off the device, move to a safe enclosed area, and avoid elevated or open locations.

Team PlayMetrics is not responsible for damage or injury caused by unsafe usage conditions.

Introduction

This manual describes the standard features and basic operation of the MMTT Hybrid Tracking Transceiver, developed by Team PlayMetrics for mission-critical communication and tracking.

Advanced configuration options may be available through authorized configuration software and are not covered in this manual.

Read this document fully before operating the device.

Communication Coverage

Under open terrain conditions, MMTT supports short-range RF communication, GSM-based long-range tracking, and offline fallback using RF or SMS.

Actual performance depends on terrain, interference, and environmental conditions.

Ingress Protection

This device is designed for operation in harsh environments and conforms to industrial ingress protection standards suitable for outdoor and field use.

GPS AND TRACKING NOTICE

Integrated GNSS and Location Module

The GNSS antenna is located beneath the top panel. Do not obstruct the top area during operation. GPS signal reception may be affected in tunnels, dense forest cover, underground areas, or metal-covered vehicles.

MMTT supports hybrid tracking using GNSS as the primary method, GSM coordinates as a secondary method, and RF fallback for offline operation.

RF SAFETY INFORMATION

MMTT emits radio frequency energy during transmission.

Maintain a minimum distance of 3 cm from the antenna while transmitting.
Do not transmit continuously for more than a 50 percent duty cycle.
Use only PlayMetrics-approved accessories.

This device is intended for professional and controlled use only.

BASIC OPERATION

Power On and Off

Rotate the power knob clockwise to switch on the device.

Volume Control

Adjust volume gradually. Avoid using earphones during volume adjustment.

Receiving

Signal strength is displayed on the screen. LED indicators show active reception.

Transmitting

Press and hold the PTT button. Speak in a normal voice from a distance of approximately 5 cm.
